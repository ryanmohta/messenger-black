Privacy

Messenger Black is committed to protecting your privacy. Sunset to Sunrise mode in Messenger Black requires your approximate location to determine the sunset and sunrise times in your specific area. By using Sunset to Sunrise mode, you consent to Messenger Black using your location approximately once per day to update your local sunset and sunrise times. This location is only used temporarily and deleted immediately after use. Your location is secure and private: Messenger Black does not store your location, and your location cannot be read by Messenger Black.

If you have any questions about how Messenger Black handles your location, please send me an email at messengerblack@ryanmohta.com; I will get back to you in one or two days.
